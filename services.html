Different problems, diverse solutions

Experience and diverse techniques are the ones that bring results time and time again.

Consulting

Emotional, mental, and psychological counseling offers a unique perspective and the ability to find alternatives to all life's challenges, whether it's career counseling and how to make better decisions from a different perspective, couple counseling, and even those aimed at improving lifestyle and personal development. I can only help you find it.

Coaching

Remember that with the appropriate aim, personal ability, desire, and a lot of self-training, you can develop in all areas of life and make yourself conquer a new goal, an old ambition, and generally enhance your lifestyle, because positive thinking produces positive reality and training tactics. You'll be able to pull more out of yourself with the use of personal validation.

Trauma

When we know how to diagnose distress and cracks from past traumas, the wound can be made to heal and recover with the help of psychotherapy and the Gestalt method. A wise man once said that the whole is different from the sum of its parts, meaning all the different layers of character and stimuli can benefit together if they are treated thoroughly and allow us to overcome all past traumas.

Psychosomatic

What isn't written about psychology anymore? So it's time to add another layer to the mental side, the emotion layer. We are sometimes driven by feelings, fears, and elements that have been with us since childhood, they testify to us and limit us; my job is to remove the layers that produce defense and reach the sympathetic point that guides us in every decision. With a lot of intention, you, too, will be able to take a step forward into a world that is healthy and barrier-free.

Sexual issues

Sexology has far-reaching effects for your genuine self and your ability to enjoy yourself. Human sexuality is the engine that drives the planet, and its appropriate utilization makes us feel loved, joyful, pleased, and content. Techniques and their implementation will help you enhance your relationship, open up and bring you closer to your partner, and most importantly, connect to yourself- mind and body.

Relationships

Welcome to the most common field, the field on which thousands of songs have been written, that surrounds us in everyday life - relationships. The human desire to feel loved and desired, satisfied and supported is accompanied by an intense need to be in a monogamous relationship, one that will improve us and make us happy. However, many relationships become destructive and produce for us the opposite. At the same time, good relationships deteriorate due to lack of attention or neglect. With proper care, relationships can improves, leading to empowerment, productivity, and the good future we so desire and deserve.
